About the app

Having love and temptation of summer sweet desserts???
Want to eat something sweet and cold? Enjoy the best sweet dessert cooking game of ice cream cupcake cone making.
Go out with a spirit to shop and make your favorite dessert to eat ice cream cone with sweet soft cupcake at the ice cream factory by yourself.
Ice cream making cone cupcake cooking game will allow you to visit the market and shop for the ingredients for ice cream and cupcake making, select items for your sweet ice cream cupcake and get eggs, flour, milk and etc for your cone dessert and become the best dessert maker in the town.
Now it's time to mix the ingredients and blend them thoroughly and then bring and pour the mixture to sweet ice cream making machine to get more colder dessert.
Select the cone and cream of your choice to have.
And at the end, have some fun with colorful and yummiest toppings on your ice cream cupcake cones.
Enjoy your sweet delicious ice cream with cupcake.

FEATURES:

- Visit to the luxury market for shopping ice cream and cake ingredients.
- Number of ingredients for different ice cream tastes and flavours for ice cream making.
- Amazing scenes of factory for mixture of ice cream cupcake cone.
- yummy cones and sweet soft cakes for different flavours.
- Exciting toppings for exciting ice cream cupcake cone making.
